About Precision Sanitation

A locally owned and operated trash pickup company dedicated to keeping Polk County clean, one pickup at a time.

Who We Are

Precision Sanitation LLC is a locally owned and operated waste collection company proudly serving Polk County, North Carolina. We were founded with a simple mission: to provide residents and businesses with dependable, affordable trash pickup they can count on every single week.

Unlike large out-of-state corporations, we're your neighbors. We live in this community, we care about it, and we take pride in showing up reliably for every customer on our routes.

We operate a comprehensive 5-day weekly pickup schedule covering all major communities in Polk County — from Columbus and Tryon to Saluda, Green Creek, Mill Spring, and the Sunny View/Lake Lure area.

Have questions, need to start service, or want to report an issue? Just call or text us at (828) 817-2307. We're here to help.
